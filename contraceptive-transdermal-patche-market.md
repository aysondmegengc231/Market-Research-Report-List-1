<p><p>The contraceptive transdermal patch market is primarily divided into two types: progestin-only contraceptives and combined hormonal contraceptives. Progestin-only patches release only the synthetic hormone progestin, suitable for individuals who may not tolerate estrogen. Combined hormonal patches contain both estrogen and progestin, providing a dual mechanism for preventing pregnancy. Each type offers distinct benefits and usage considerations, appealing to different demographics based on health needs, lifestyle preferences, and contraceptive efficacy.</p></p>
