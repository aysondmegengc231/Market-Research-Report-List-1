<p><p>The Lyme Disease Diagnostic Devices market encompasses several testing methods. Serological tests detect antibodies to the Lyme bacteria in the blood, while urine antigen tests identify bacterial antigens in urine samples. The lymphocytic transformation test measures immune response through lymphocyte activation. Immunofluorescent staining uses fluorescent dyes to visualize Lyme bacteria. Nucleic acid tests amplify and detect genetic material from the bacteria, providing accurate results. Each method offers unique advantages for diagnosing Lyme disease, contributing to improved patient management.</p></p>
